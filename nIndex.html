Hello

My name is Tyler Mondragon and this is my portfolio website. I love to design, draw, code, and develope new and creative things that just look cool. In this website you'll find my designs that were created in Adobe Illustrator and apps I have developed in my Mobile App and design class, but I'll be refering to that as M.A.A.D..

From zany cartoons to intense action or horror sequences, I see something creative and surreal that I can create. For example, I can image monkeys jumping up and down on a teacher's desk or cupcake monsters taking over my notebooks. I use these thoughts as inspiration. I see something creative or humorous in almost everything. I used my imagination to create funny stories. Just picturing what could be created and how funny things would look like when I image my art. This is when art is most interesting to me.
